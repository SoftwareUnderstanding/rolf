# PyTorch Insightface

Reimplement of [insightface](https://github.com/deepinsight/insightface) face recognition algorithm.

## How to Train
````
python3 train.py
````

## How to Verification
````
python3 verification.py
````

## Dataset

- Train Dataset
  - face_emore_glint

- **Val Dataset**
  - LFW
  - CFP_FP
  - AgeDB30

## Reference

- **insightface repo**: [insightface](https://github.com/deepinsight/insightface)
- **ArcFace: Additive Angular Margin Loss for Deep Face Recognition**([arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/1801.07698))
