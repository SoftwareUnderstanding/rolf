# MCMC
practice exercises in MCMC

1. Bayes logit polya-gammma - MH for Bayes Logit Regression and Gibbs sampler using data augmentation as proposed in https://arxiv.org/pdf/1205.0310.pdf

2. MCMC normal - MCMC outputs from Normal target with varying proposals

3. MCMC multivariate normal - MCMC algorithms for multivariate normal, understanding the impact of shrinking proposal

4. MHWG linchpin - Metropolis Hasting within Gibbs and linchpin variable sampler

5. MH Gibbs - Comparing Gibbs and MH for bivariate normal with varying degree of correlation

6. Deterministic scan MH - Deterministic Scan MH for 100 dimensional normal
