# W-gan-Simple
Simple Implementation of WGAN for MNIST or other datasets
An alternative to traditional GAN training, In this model, the authors have improved the stability of learning, 
eliminated mode collapse, and have provided meaningful learning curves useful for debugging and hyperparameter searches.
