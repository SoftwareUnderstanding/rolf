# [Re] Powerpropagation: A sparsity inducing weight reparameterisation

*A Georgia Tech CS 7643 Project*

A re-implementation of [Powerpropagation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2110.00296v2), a NeurIPS 2021 paper by Schwarz, Jonathan, et al. in Pytorch.

### References

Jonathan Schwarz, Siddhant M Jayakumar, Razvan Pascanu, Peter Latham, and Yee Whye Teh. Powerpropagation: A sparsity inducing weight reparameterisation. *arXiv preprint arXiv:2110.00296*, 2021.
