# Vision Transformer
A TensorFlow and PyTorch implementation of Vision Transformers (ViT) as described by [An Image is Worth 16x16 words: Transformers for Image Recognition at Scale - Alexey Dosovitskiy et al.](https://arxiv.org/abs/2010.11929)
