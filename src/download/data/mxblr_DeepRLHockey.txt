# DeepRLHockey

## Task
Training a Deep Reinforcement Learning Agent on the Laser-Hockey-Environment (https://github.com/martius-lab/laser-hockey-env)

## Agents
### Soft-Actor-Critic
Implementation of the Soft-Actor-Critic algorithm by Tuomas Haarnoja, Aurick Zhou, Pieter Abbeel and Sergey Levine (https://arxiv.org/abs/1801.01290)
