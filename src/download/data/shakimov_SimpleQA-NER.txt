Named Entity Recognition and Linking for SimpleQA dataset (code adapted from another <a href="https://github.com/kamalkraj/Named-Entity-Recognition-with-Bidirectional-LSTM-CNNs">repo</a>)

A keras implementation of Bidirectional-LSTM_CNNs for Named-Entity-Recoganition. The original paper can be found at https://arxiv.org/abs/1511.08308
