iSEDfit
=======

iSEDfit is a Bayesian spectral energy distribution fitting code designed to
extract the physical properties of galaxies from broadband photometry.  The full
suite of routines are publicly distributed as part of my
[impro](https://github.com/moustakas/impro) IDL library.
