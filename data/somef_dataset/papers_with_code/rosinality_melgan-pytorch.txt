# melgan-pytorch

MelGAN: Generative Adversarial Networks for Conditional Waveform Synthesis (https://arxiv.org/abs/1910.06711) and Natural TTS Synthesis by Conditioning WaveNet on Mel Spectrogram Predictions (https://arxiv.org/abs/1712.05884) in PyTorch

Currently model can generate mel spectrograms and wavs.
