## ResNet

This is a PyTorch implementation of a vanilla ResNet-50. The ResNet-50 can be easily adapted to make it into a ResNet101 or ResNet152. I have included these models which tend to perform better on image classification tasks than the ResNet-50 but at a higher computational cost.

For those who are interested, here is the ResNet Paper: :fire: https://arxiv.org/abs/1512.03385 :fire:
